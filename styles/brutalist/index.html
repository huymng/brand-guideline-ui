RULES

004

THESE ARE GUIDELINES. BREAK THEM IF YOU WANT.

DO:

  • → USE HIGH CONTRAST
  • → MAKE TEXT READABLE
  • → BE INTENTIONALLY UGLY
  • → USE SYSTEM FONTS
  • → EMBRACE ASYMMETRY
  • → MAKE IT FUNCTIONAL

DON'T:

  • × ROUND YOUR CORNERS
  • × USE SUBTLE GRADIENTS
  • × ADD UNNECESSARY ANIMATION
  • × MAKE IT "PRETTY"
  • × FOLLOW TRENDS BLINDLY
